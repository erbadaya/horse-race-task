# Horses that are expected to win (from previous races)

ANGELS' SHARE is the clear pick and has strong claims of kicking off her handicap career with a nursery win here. William Knight's filly landed her first victory last time over CD storming home eight lengths clear of the field. She can race of an 11lb lower mark than she will in the future and should be incredibly tough to beat.

This can go to DUE WEST who has posted four solid career efforts and can go one better than the last twice when runner-up. The latest defeat came on nursery handicap debut when just held by a neck with the third 5l adrift of the front pair. He's very well-treated on that, and is due to go up 3lbs in the future, and proven under the conditions has a great oportunity to open his account. 

GOUROU (8) is forced to take on some good younger horses but he showed he was up to the task when imposing here in early-July and his Vincennes run stands him in good stead. GASOLIN (3) showed recently that he is close to his race and enjoys a better spot behind the car. Back to the provinces after having bitten off too much at Enghien last time, NICE PRESENT (6) should prove significantly more competitive this time. HERCULE DE LEAU (7) has hardly put a foot wrong this term and placed last time at the venue. Oddly below-par at Amiens, HUNT (5) has otherwise been in fine form and must not be underestimated.

MAKIAVEL DE BUSSET (6) is new to racing left-handed but looks a thorough professional and can secure a first hat-trick.

One World filly MARKETA has decent form and has had a run at Hollywoodbets Durbanville so should have every chance. CALL OF THE KAROO can also get it right after finishing runner-up. She is feature placed and can only improve in her second run after a rest. PENNYS CHOICE made good improvement with blinkers last time and can take it to the wire again. More can improve and there are first-timers to keep an eye on.

Highly regarded KING'S QUEST is in good nick and gets a go at 1800m. It looks the right race. 

PAPA'S NICO BOY scored comfortably over C&D last time out and he looks capable of going in again.

GIFTED VALOR has shown up well in recent workouts and he could be primed to strike first time out.
